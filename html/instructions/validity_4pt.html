VALIDITY

Conversations consist of a sequence of turns, alternating between two participants. Your task: Does this last turn make sense with respect to the previous turns? You will be given between 1-3 previous turns to make your judgement. You will rate whether the last sentence is a valid response, given the prior turns, Do so on the following scale:

4-level scale:

  • Invalid response
  • Somewhat acceptable response
  • Probably acceptable response
  • Valid response

Example

1 (invalid response):

Example 1

 U1: Do you know google? {CONTEXT}
U2: Uh-huh, I do. {CONTEXT}
U1: who is google? {CONTEXT}
U2: Not necessarily.

Example 2

 U1: so what's up today? {CONTEXT}
U2: they lived together {CONTEXT}
U1: who lived together? and when? {CONTEXT}
U2: where's Gordon has he changed

2 (Somewhat acceptable response):

Example 1

 U1: i said no {CONTEXT}
U2: you said no to who and to what {CONTEXT}
U1: from morning edition {CONTEXT}
U2: hmmm waking-up is hard.

Example 2

 U1: Ok, I see. {CONTEXT}
U2: Where are you going? {CONTEXT}
U1: To the moon! {CONTEXT}
U2: That's good. Reassure me.

3 (Probably acceptable response):

Example 1

 U1: I like both actually. {CONTEXT}
U2: Do you watch them with your kids? {CONTEXT}
U1: I don't have kids. {CONTEXT}
U2: let us make a plan to see the latest star war movies together

Example 2

 U1: oh good i'm so glad {CONTEXT}
U2: yeah me too {CONTEXT}
U1: you're supposed to {CONTEXT}
U2: sure, what's the weather like?
