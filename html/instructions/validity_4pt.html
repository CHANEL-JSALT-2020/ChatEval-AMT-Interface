VALIDITY

Conversations consist of a sequence of turns, alternating between two participants. Your task: Does this last turn make sense with respect to the previous turns? You will be given between 1-3 previous turns to make your judgement. You will rate whether the last sentence is a valid response, given the prior turns, Do so on the following scale:

4-level scale:

  • Invalid response
  • Somewhat acceptable response
  • Probably acceptable response
  • Valid response

Examples

1 (invalid response):

  
Example 1
U1: Do you know google? context
U2: Uh-huh, I do.  
U1: who is google?  
U2: Not necessarily. ◄ rate this
Example 2
U1: so what's up today? context
U2: they lived together  
U1: who lived together? and when?  
U2: where's Gordon has he changed ◄ rate this

2 (Somewhat acceptable response):

  
Example 1
U1: i said no context
U2: you said no to who and to what  
U1: from morning edition  
U2: hmmm waking-up is hard. ◄ rate this
Example 2
U1: Ok, I see. context
U2: Where are you going?  
U1: To the moon!  
U2: That's good. Reassure me. ◄ rate this

3 (Probably acceptable response):

  
Example 1
U1: I like both actually. context
U2: Do you watch them with your kids?  
U1: I don't have kids.  
U2: let us make a plan to see the latest star war movies together ◄ rate this
Example 2
U1: oh good i'm so glad context
U2: yeah me too  
U1: you're supposed to  
U2: sure, what's the weather like? ◄ rate this
